Background

Choleric, Sanguine, Phlegmatic, Melancholic: the four temperaments that define the societal divide in Valverde, the perfect city with the perfect system. The annual Testing Day has arrived, and all citizens are invited to mingle and celebrate as the next generation discovers their place in the world. Youâ€™re not a person until you know the path the city has chosen for you.

After the testing but before the results are announced, there will be a knighting and an execution-- in that order. Something has gone not quite right this year, and no one wants to say why.

All news of the revolution has been suppressed.

Valverde is crumbling.

Inspired by teen dystopian fiction in the vein of the Hunger Games, Divergent, Shade's Children, and the Bioshock series, Brick and Water is an R16 game for up to 20 players.
